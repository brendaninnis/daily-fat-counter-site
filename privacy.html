Privacy Policy

Daily Fat Counter does not collect anything, track anything or do anything that is not a daily fat counter.

The app stores your daily fat history and reset time in a local file on your device. Daily Fat Counter has no access to the internet and does not send this file anywhere or make any copies of it. Files within the app are protected from access from any other app by the app sandbox. Daily Fat Counter is built with simplicity and privacy from the start.

This website uses Google Analytics to analyze traffic and usage of this website in order to improve this website to better promote Daily Fat Counter.
